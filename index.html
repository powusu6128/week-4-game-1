Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of rupees to your wallet.

You win the game by matching your total amount of crystals to the random number, you lose the game if your total amount of crystals goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
